Tooltip

Sometimes you need to show a simple description for an element on the page. For that, you can use the Tooltip Component:

I am THE tooltip!
I do have a tooltip.
Example: HTML inline code <a data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div role="tooltip">   I am THE tooltip!  </div>  I do have a tooltip. </a>

As you can see, we just use some element - in that case a button component - with our custom data-css-group to give the tooltip a wrapper and, in that example, the ARIA role="tooltip" attribute for the tooltip.
The data-css-group="tooltip" is mandatory, to simplify the CSS.

Being a HTML element, the tooltip accepts any content, but you should avoid to put a lot of thing inside this component, for it's made to be a simple way to provide quick extra information to the user.

Notice: Technically you can use multiple tooltips on an element, as the wrapper attribute name (data-css-group) implies, but its a very bad pratice that sould never be done.

Sides

You can choose the position of the tooltip with the data-css-side attribute on the tooltip. There are four directions to choose:

  • top (default)
  • right
  • bottom
  • left
I am on top
On top
I am on right
On right
I am on bottom
On bottom
I am on left
On left
Example: HTML inline code <a data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div role="tooltip">   I am on top  </div>  On top </a> <a data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div role="tooltip" data-css-side="right">   I am on right  </div>  On right </a> <a data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div role="tooltip" data-css-side="bottom">   I am on bottom  </div>  On bottom </a> <a data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div role="tooltip" data-css-side="left">   I am on left  </div>  On left </a>

As the top position is the default, it's the only one that doesn't use the data-css-side attribute.

Accent Colors

Like any other of our components, tooltips can also be used with your chosen accent color:

I am on top
On top
I am on right
On right
Example: HTML inline code <a data-css-accent data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div data-css-accent role="tooltip">   I am on top  </div>  On top </a> <a data-css-accent data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div data-css-accent data-css-side="right" role="tooltip">   I am on right  </div>  On right </a>

Different Colors

A tooltip may use a different color pallete from its wrapper element, but remeber our convention: if the component doesn' state that it will use the accent color (by the data-css-accent attribute), the primary color will be used.

I'm on accent color
I'm on primary color
I do use primary color
I do use accent color
Example: HTML inline code <a data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div data-css-accent role="tooltip">   I'm on accent color  </div>  I'm on primary color </a> <a data-css-accent data-css-group="tooltip" role="button">  <div role="tooltip">   I do use primary color  </div>  I do use accent color </a>
